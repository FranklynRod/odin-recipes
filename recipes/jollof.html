Jollof

Description

This is my favorite dish that my mother cooks. Jollof hails form the western region of Africa. This dish is home to a few countries within this region including Ghana and Nigeria. Jollof is a hefty meal of a tomato based rice, beef, and regional spices. It's considered a party dish; feel free to take it to your next gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Jasmine rice
  • 3/4 cup of cubed grass-fed beef
  • 1/2 cup of tomato paste
  • 1 diced onion
  • 2 habenero peppers
  • 1 tablespoon of oil of choice (olive, palm)
  • Optional: 1 cup of mixed veggies
  • Water or chicken broth
  • West African Seasoning

Tools

  • Blender
  • Large Deep Pot
  • Tasting Spoon
  • Cooking Spoon

Steps

  1. Add oil into pot and then toss in onions
  2. Add beef once onions are light brown. Then add seasoning
  3. Taste for spiciness and meat texture
  4. Stir in rice, broth
