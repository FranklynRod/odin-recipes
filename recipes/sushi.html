Sushi

Description

This is my go to date night restaurants dish. If you're a fan of Japanese cuisine; you're going ot love this. These small bite sizes of fatty raw salmon and rice are the perfect dinner idea.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Sushi safe fish (salmon or tuna)
  • Avocado
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sushi seaweed
  • Cucumber

Tools

  • Sharp knife
  • Bamboo mat
  • Dipping sauce bowl

Steps

  1. Prepare your sushi rice.
  2. Lay your seaweed onto the mat.
  3. Layer rice on the seaweed. Make sure it is flat
  4. Add fish, avocado, cucumber
  5. Cut sushi into 4 inch bit sizes.
  6. Dip into soy sauce and enjoy
